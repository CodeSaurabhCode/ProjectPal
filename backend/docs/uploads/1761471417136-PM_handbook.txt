PROJECT MANAGEMENT HANDBOOK

Budget Approval Guidelines:
- Projects under $10,000: Team Lead approval required
- Projects $10,000 - $50,000: Department Manager approval required  
- Projects over $50,000: VP approval required
- Projects over $500,000: Executive Board approval required

Resource Allocation:
- Engineering Leads must have 40+ hours available weekly
- Designers require 20+ hours for new projects
- All staff assignments require HR directory check

Kick-off Requirements:
- Project ticket must be created within 24 hours
- Initial team meeting scheduled within 72 hours
- Budget approval documentation attached to ticket
